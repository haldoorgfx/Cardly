Amina Osman

Founder & CEO, Sahel Pay
About

Amina founded Sahel Pay in 2019 to build payments rails for the Horn of Africa, now processing over $400M annually across four countries. Before that she led growth at a pan-African fintech and spent five years in mobile money. She speaks on building for the next billion users, fundraising on the continent, and the operational realities of scaling across borders.
