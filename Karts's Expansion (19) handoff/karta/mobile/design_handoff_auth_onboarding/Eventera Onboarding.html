Eventera · Attendee
Onboarding & profile setup
A short, skippable wizard right after signup that captures the information Eventera needs to personalize the experience — and that organizers need to run a great event. Every step says why it's asked. Collected once, reused everywhere: your attending card, AI networking matches, agenda recommendations, and the organizer's headcount for catering & accessibility. Basics are required; the rest is optional and editable later in Profile.
