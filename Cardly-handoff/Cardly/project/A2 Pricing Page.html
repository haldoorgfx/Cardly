FAQ

Questions, briefly answered.

What counts as one "event"?+

One event = one design + one public link. You can keep an event open indefinitely; cards generated by attendees during that time don't count as new events.

Do attendees need an account?+

No. The link is the product — your attendees just open it on their phone, fill in their info, and download. No login, no friction.

Can I change plans later?+

Anytime. Upgrades are prorated, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?+

Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), Apple Pay, Google Pay. Annual plans can be invoiced.

Is there an education / NGO discount?+

Yes — 50% off any paid plan. Email us with proof and we'll set it up the same day.
