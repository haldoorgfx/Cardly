## DESIGN RULES (same as previous sessions)

- DM Sans 400 with -0.025em tracking on display sizes
- JetBrains Mono for ALL numbers, prices, stats
- Gold (#E8C57E) appears ONCE per section as the accent moment
- Cream canvas (#FAF6EE) — never white #FFFFFF as page background
- 1px hairline borders on cards, never heavy shadows
- Photography bleeds edge-to-edge on all event and use-case cards
- Forest fills on primary CTAs
- The Karta Card visual always has the gold glow treatment — it's the product's signature moment
- No generic SaaS clichés: no rocket icons, no blue gradients, no confetti illustrations
- The site feels editorial and African-forward, not generic Silicon Valley SaaS
